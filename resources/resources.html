Uncle Poopy's Life Resources

Life is hard. Uncle Poopy has tips, tricks, and backup.

Tap a Topic

Make sure to check your school’s course catalog and meet with your advisor. Don’t forget these resources:

  • DegreeWorks - Track your degree progress
  • Rate My Professors - See what others think
  • Check your university's add/drop deadlines!

Healthy communication and boundaries matter more than cheesy pickup lines.

  • Love Is Respect – Help with dating and consent
  • Uncle Poopy says: “Don’t text your ex after midnight. Or ever, really.”
  • Try BYU-I’s Counseling Center for advice or workshops

You're not alone. Please reach out — there is help and healing.

  • Call or Text 988 – Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
  • NAMI.org – National Alliance on Mental Illness
  • BYU-I Crisis Help

Time-blocking saves lives (and grades).

  • Google Calendar – Set reminders and recurring events
  • Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 mins work, 5 mins break
  • Todoist – Task management done right

Being broke doesn’t mean eating badly.

  • Budget Bytes – Cheap, tasty recipes
  • Make a shopping list and stick to it (don't shop hungry)
  • Invest in rice, beans, eggs, and frozen veggies. Lifesavers.
  • SNAP-Ed – Help with food budgeting and assistance
